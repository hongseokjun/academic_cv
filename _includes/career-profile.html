Profile

I am a neuroimager having interests in typical and atypical brain development. My research focus has been advanced multimodal imaging on malformations of cortical development, and now I seek to broaden my scope to other disorders such as Autism and ADHD. Applying a rich array of structural and functional MRI and connectome markers, my overall research goal aims at comprehensive understanding of large-scale pathogeneic mechanisms of neurodevelopmental disorders and associating these macro-level knowledge to underlying genetic substrates.
